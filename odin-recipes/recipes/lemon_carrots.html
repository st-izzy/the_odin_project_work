Lemon-Glazed Carrots

Very easy and quick to make!

Total Time: 20 min | Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 2 carrots, sliced 1/4-inch thick
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Place carrots into a large pot and cover with water;
    bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer
    until carrots are tender, about 8 minutes. Drain.
  2. Heat butter in a skillet over medium heat; cook and
    stir carrots, brown sugar, and lemon juice in the
    melted butter, stirring often, until sugar has
    dissolved, 2 minutes.
